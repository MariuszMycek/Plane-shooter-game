ALIEN SHOOTER

by Mariusz Mycek

Attention! - clear the cache after the new version is released.

Controls:

Press SPACE to shot and accept option

Press UP, DOWN arrows to change menu option

Press LEFT, RIGHT arrows to move the plane

Press P to pause the game

Enemies:

A basic alien landing ship. It can't shoot, but when it crosses the line of defense, you lose one life.

Weapons:

Single bullet cannon - a basic weapon for your ship. Max amount of bullets at the same time - 15 pcs.

Double bullet cannon - improved version of single bullet cannon. Shoots 2 bullets at the same time. Max amount of bullets at the same time - 24 pcs.

Piercing single bullet cannon - improved version of single bullet cannon. Shoots 1 piercing bullet at the same time. Max amount of bullets at the same time - 16 pcs.

Bonuses:

Sometimes they lie on the ground. They contain random bonus weapon for your ship. But be careful - if you lose your life, you also lose your bonus weapon.
